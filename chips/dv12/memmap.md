| 0x1F70000 - 0x1F7FFFF | 64k    | HSB peripherals       |
| 0x1FA0000 - 0x1FB7FFF | 96k    | VRAM                  |
| 0x2000000 - 0x27FFFFF | 8M     | SDRAM                 |
